ABOUT

A website that will help you explore good opportunities to build your career A place where you can get information about career-building opportunities This site will give the latest news related to tech jobs and other educational news.
Techclix provides the path and information for students who want to get ahead in this battle for success. It provides you with all the upcoming hackathons, internships, and jobs.
Techclix will help users create a professional presence and build a resume that showcases their skills and knowledge. It will also provide you with the schedules and venues for seminars and webinars and give you a link to directly apply from the site.
Moreover, the website will give you notifications for every update in the world of tech and non-tech fields. It also provides you with a guided path to prepare for upcoming events that will help you prepare for them and ace them.
